1 T24 initial condition with customer account opening functionality. Use-Case: Account Opening with Additional Customer Details
In the Account Opening screen:
  1. Input customer details like name, address, etc.
  2. Input additional customer details like occupation, employer, income source, etc.
  3. Select account type and other account-related details
  4. Review and submit the application
 Expected Result:
The system should allow capturing additional customer details beyond the basic information during account opening.
After submission, the customer's account should be created with all details recorded.
2 Customer has an existing account in T24. Use-Case: Update Existing Customer Details
In the Customer Details screen:
  1. Search and select an existing customer
  2. Modify customer details like address, contact information, etc.
  3. Submit the changes
 Expected Result:
The system should allow updating customer details for an existing customer record.
After submission, the customer's updated information should be reflected in their account records.
3 Initial T24 configuration without customer screening functionality. Use-Case: Customer Screening and Risk Assessment
During the Account Opening process:
  1. Input customer details
  2. Trigger customer screening process
  3. System performs screening against watch lists, risk databases, etc.
  4. Display screening results with risk level
  5. Proceed with account opening based on screening outcome
 Expected Result:
The system should perform customer screening and risk assessment as part of the account opening process.
Based on the screening results and risk level, the system should either allow or restrict account opening.
